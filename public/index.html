Leemon Ikpea Foundation Remains Ever Devoted To Helping The Less Privileged

With the preponderance of irresolute platforms parading as Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) particularly those that are said to have been designed to cater for the needy, the Leemon Ikpea Foundation stands out as a beacon of hope. The foundation has consistently demonstrated unwavering commitment to helping the less privileged, providing scholarships, healthcare, and empowerment programs. Its transparent operations and genuine concern for humanity have earned it widespread respect and trust. Beneficiaries recount stories of transformation, attributing their progress to the foundation’s timely interventions and support. The Leemon Ikpea Foundation remains ever devoted to uplifting lives and fostering sustainable development in communities across Nigeria.

My Encounter With Alif

I can't forget the year 2016 making it over 5 years I left secondary school... Obviously the dream of going to the university was there...

I can't forget the year 2016 making it over 5 years I left secondary school. Obviously, the dream of going to the university was there, but financial constraints made it seem impossible. When I attended the ALIF mentoring session, I was inspired and encouraged. The foundation analyzed my needs and made a recommendation to the Board of Trustees. Through ALIF’s grant, I was able to pursue my university education. Today, I am a proud graduate, and I owe my success to the support and guidance provided by ALIF. My encounter with ALIF changed my life, and I am forever grateful for the opportunity and hope it gave me.
