We dedicate this Foundation to our late parents, Johnson and Iluaria Ikpea in honour of their memory. ALIF was officially inaugurated on November 17, 2012 with sincere desire to benefit humanity through charitable activities.

Chief (Dr.) Leemon A. Ikpea envisioned ALIF as a beacon of hope, inspired by his parents' legacy of compassion and service. The foundation strives to empower individuals through education and healthcare.

WHAT WE ARE MADE OF

ALIF was officially inaugurated with sincere desire to benefit humanity through charitable activities which includes:

Scholarship

  • Extending scholarship scheme to indigent students and young persons of school age, especially orphans.

Health

  • Catering for the sick and aged.

Youth Development

  • Promoting youth employment through skills acquisition.

Rural Health Care Services

  • Ensuring easy access to primary health care services for people living in rural communities.
