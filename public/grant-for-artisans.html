Grant for Artisans

Our grant program supports artisans by providing financial assistance to enhance their skills and grow their businesses. We aim to empower artisans to achieve sustainable livelihoods.

Main Photo
Photo 1
Photo 2

Who Can Benefit?

  • Tailors, carpenters, welders, and other skilled tradespeople
  • Young people seeking to learn a trade or start a small business
  • Women artisans looking to expand their craft or cooperative
  • Groups and associations working to uplift their members

What the Grant Covers

  • Purchase of essential tools and equipment
  • Training and capacity building workshops
  • Support for business registration and branding
  • Mentorship and networking opportunities

Stories of Impact

“Before receiving the ALIF artisan grant, I struggled to buy the tools I needed for my carpentry business. Now, I have a steady stream of customers and have even trained two apprentices.” — Samuel, Carpenter

“The grant helped our women’s cooperative purchase sewing machines and attend a workshop on fashion design. We now supply uniforms to local schools.” — Fatima, Tailor

How to Get Involved

  1. Stay tuned for our next grant application window.
  2. Prepare a simple proposal describing your craft and how the grant will help you grow.
  3. Attend our information sessions and workshops for tips on applying.
  4. Follow us on social media for updates and success stories.

ALIF was officially inaugurated on November 17, 2012, with a sincere desire to benefit humanity through structured charitable activities.
