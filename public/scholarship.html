Investing in Academic Excellence

Empowering Dreams Through Education

The ALIF Foundation Scholarship is more than just financial support—it's a commitment to nurturing the next generation of leaders, innovators, and change-makers. We believe that every child, regardless of background, deserves the opportunity to pursue their academic dreams and reach their full potential.

Our scholarship program has transformed the lives of countless students, enabling them to attend school, excel in their studies, and give back to their communities. From covering tuition fees to providing mentorship and resources, ALIF stands by our scholars every step of the way.

“I never thought I could go to university, but the ALIF scholarship made it possible. Now, I’m studying to become a doctor and hope to help others in my community.” — Blessing, Scholarship Recipient

“The support from ALIF Foundation gave me the confidence to pursue my dreams. I am grateful for the opportunity to learn and grow.” — Daniel, Beneficiary

Scholarship Guidelines

01. General Overview

Education is the cornerstone of progress. Our scholarships aim to uplift underprivileged students by providing financial aid and mentorship opportunities.

02. Eligibility

  • Proof of indigent status or orphanhood.
  • Academic transcripts with a minimum GPA.
  • Letter of recommendation from a community leader or school principal.

03. Documentation

Ensure all required documents are submitted in PDF format. Missing documents may result in disqualification.

04. Selection Process

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview with the Board of Trustees. Final selection will be based on merit and need.
