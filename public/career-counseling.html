Career Counseling

Our career counseling program is designed to help you discover your strengths, set meaningful goals, and pursue a fulfilling career path. Whether you’re a student, graduate, or artisan looking to upskill, ALIF provides personalized guidance, mentorship, and resources to help you succeed in today’s world.

What We Offer

  • One-on-one career guidance sessions
  • Workshops on CV writing, interview skills, and job search strategies
  • Mentorship from professionals in various fields
  • Access to internship and apprenticeship opportunities
  • Support for entrepreneurship and business planning

Real Impact

“I was unsure about my future after school. ALIF’s counseling helped me discover my passion for technology, and now I’m working as a web developer.” — Joy, Graduate

“The interview workshop gave me the confidence to land my first job. Thank you, ALIF!” — Michael, Youth Beneficiary

Empowering Youth for the Future

Our career counseling program goes beyond advice—it’s about building confidence and opening doors. We host youth empowerment events, connect participants with mentors, and provide practical skills for real-world success.

  3. Connect with a mentor in your field of interest.
  4. Take the next step toward your dream career!
