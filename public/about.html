THIS IS ALIF

Welcome to Agbonjagwe Leemon Ikpea Foundation

Our mission is to uplift lives through education, healthcare, and community development, ensuring that every individual has the opportunity to reach their full potential.

To be a Beacon of Hope

We are dedicated to providing life-changing scholarships to indigent students and orphans, while ensuring rural communities have access to the healthcare they deserve.

A Future of Shared Prosperity

To see a Nigeria where every young person is empowered through skill acquisition and every elder is cared for with dignity.

Core Values

Empathy

Catering to the sick and aged.

Integrity

Analyzing needs through the Board of Trustees to ensure grants go to those truly in need.

Impact

Promoting youth employment through tangible skills.
